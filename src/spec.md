# Specification

## Summary
**Goal:** Create a professional single-page homepage for Jonathan Ayan, a life and annuity insurance broker in Miami, Florida.

**Planned changes:**
- Build homepage with hero section introducing Jonathan Ayan and Miami location
- Add services section explaining life insurance and annuities offerings
- Display section showcasing A-rated insurance companies (Mutual of Omaha, FNG, Transamerica, Ethos, Forrester)
- Include contact information section
- Apply professional design with navy blue, gold accents, and white color scheme

**User-visible outcome:** Visitors see a professional insurance broker homepage with Jonathan Ayan's introduction, services offered, trusted insurance companies represented, and contact information.
